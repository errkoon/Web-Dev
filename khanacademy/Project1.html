Travel to Niagara Fall and Mountain Matterhorn

Niagara Falls is a world-renowned, massive collection of three waterfalls—Horseshoe Falls, American Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls—straddling the border between Canada and the USA

The Matterhornis a 4,478-meter (14,692 ft) pyramid-shaped peak on the Swiss-Italian border, famous as a landmark of the Alps and Switzerland. It is known for its iconic, isolated shape, often referred to as the "Toblerone mountain" and as a "mountain of mountains".
